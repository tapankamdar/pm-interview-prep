# PM Mock Question Library

Questions organized by type and seniority. Select based on company archetype and candidate level.

---

## Product Sense / Design

**IC / Senior PM:**
- "Design a feature to help Uber drivers earn more during slow periods."
- "How would you improve Google Maps for commuters?"
- "You're PM for Instagram DMs. What would you build next and why?"

**Director / VP:**
- "How would you think about Instacart's AI product strategy for the next 3 years?"
- "You're the new VP of Product at a travel company. What's the first thing you'd change and why?"
- "Design a product that helps small businesses compete with Amazon."

**AI-first variant:**
- "How would you design an AI feature for [company's core product] that users would actually trust?"
- "What's the hardest part of making AI useful in [domain] — and how would you solve it?"

---

## Strategy / Vision

**All levels:**
- "How would you grow [product] from 10M to 100M users?"
- "A major competitor just launched a product that directly competes with your core offering. What do you do?"
- "How would you prioritize your roadmap if you had to cut 50% of your team?"

**Director+ only:**
- "Walk me through how you'd build your product vision for the first 90 days."
- "How do you decide when to build vs. buy vs. partner?"
- "What's the most important thing [company] should work on in the next 18 months that they're probably not doing?"

---

## Metrics / Analytics

**All levels:**
- "How would you measure the success of [feature / product]?"
- "[Metric] dropped 15% last Tuesday. Walk me through how you'd diagnose it."
- "What's the north star metric for [product] and why?"

**Senior PM+:**
- "DAU is flat but revenue is growing. Is that good or bad?"
- "You've launched a new feature and 30% of users are using it, but NPS hasn't moved. What's going on?"
- "How do you know when a metric is the right one vs. a proxy?"

---

## Execution / Delivery / Leadership

**All levels:**
- "Tell me about a time you had to ship something you didn't fully believe in. How did you handle it?"
- "Tell me about a time a launch didn't go as planned. What happened and what did you do?"
- "How do you align engineering when they push back on your priorities?"

**Director+:**
- "Tell me about a time you had to say no to a request from a senior stakeholder. How did you handle it?"
- "Tell me about building a PM team from scratch. What did you get right and what would you do differently?"
- "Walk me through a time when you had to change the direction of a major initiative mid-stream."

---

## Technical Depth / AI (AI-first companies)

**All levels:**
- "How do you evaluate whether an AI feature is ready to ship?"
- "Walk me through how you'd build the eval infrastructure for a new LLM-powered feature."
- "What's the difference between an AI product that users trust vs. one they abandon?"

**Senior PM+:**
- "How do you think about the tradeoffs between model capability and product reliability?"
- "Your AI feature is 90% accurate. Is that good enough to ship? How do you decide?"
- "How would you explain to a GC or enterprise buyer why they should trust your AI's output?"

---

## Calibration by Seniority

| Seniority | Emphasis | What they're calibrating |
|---|---|---|
| IC / APM | Product craft, user empathy, execution | Can they do the PM job? |
| Senior PM | Depth + breadth, independent decisions | Can they own a product area? |
| Director | Org navigation, systems thinking, team leadership | Can they lead a PM function? |
| VP / GM | P&L thinking, executive presence, vision | Can they run a business? |
